About

id: 01 · class: profile

I prepare the datasets that sit underneath AI systems before anyone calls them "intelligent." That means image, video, text, and audio annotation — bounding boxes, polygons, segmentation, classification, OCR tagging — done to a standard that holds up under quality review, not just at first glance.

Most of my work happens inside Human-in-the-Loop environments, where annotation and model output feed each other in real time. I've supported domain-specific labeling for a medical AI chatbot backend, led a small annotation team, and trained new annotators on tools and guidelines so accuracy doesn't drop when the workload scales.

I hold a B.A. in English Literature — which, unglamorously, is exactly why my text annotation and QA notes read clearly and my labeling guidelines don't get misread by the next person on the project.
